Privacy Policy

Quick Summary

Docify respects your privacy. All file processing happens locally on your device. We do NOT send your files to any external servers.

1. Information We Collect

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your data. Information we collect:

  • Files you choose: For conversion and processing only (processed locally on your device)
  • Usage statistics: Anonymous information to improve the app
  • App settings: Your personal preferences
  • Error logs: To help solve technical issues

2. How We Use Your Information

We use data only to provide better service:

  1. Provide file conversion services (PDF, ZIP, OCR)
  2. Improve app performance and stability
  3. Fix bugs and solve problems
  4. Develop new features based on needs
  5. Ensure app security and protect your data

3. Required Permissions

Permission Explanation

  • Storage (Read/Write): To open and save files you choose
  • Camera: To scan documents using OCR
  • Internet (Optional): For ads only, not for your files

Note: All files stay on your device. We access your personal files only when you choose them yourself.

4. Data Protection

Your Data Security

We are committed to protecting your data:

  • Local Processing: All conversions happen on your device
  • No Storage: We don't keep copies of your files
  • No Sharing: We don't sell or rent your data to anyone
  • Encryption: We encrypt sensitive data when stored

5. Cookies & Technologies

We use simple technologies to improve your experience:

  • Local storage cache (for your settings)
  • Anonymous usage statistics
  • We don't use tracking cookies

6. Your Rights

You have full rights to:

  • Know what data we collect
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Refuse to grant permissions (with reduced features)
  • Opt-out of ads

7. Policy Changes

We may update this policy. We'll notify you about important changes via:

  • In-app notification
  • Update to this webpage
  • Email (if you provided one)
